## Best Options

Teaching MS from state school (in-state tuition)

- Rather than teaching certificate
- Pays slightly more than just teaching certification

### UW 

[Apply here!](https://apply.grad.uw.edu/portal/gr_app)

1. [UW Accelerated Certification for Teachers (U-ACT)](https://education.uw.edu/academics/program/u-act)
    - Deadline: May 15, 2026
    - Duration: 1-2 years
    - Online, Part-time
    - One evening online class per week, usually on Tuesday or Wednesday
        - Additional online class is held bimonthly, typically on a Thursday
    - Total: $28,550

2. [Secondary Teacher Education Program (STEP)](https://education.uw.edu/academics/program/secondary-teacher-education)
    - 1 year
    - Field based, On-campus
    - Full-time
    - October 10, 2025, but ongoing applications
    - Washington state residents: $6,912 per quarter, 4 quarters
    - $27,648
