# Skateboard Deck

Most made from Hard "rock" Maple grown in NA.

## Make your own steps

1. Buy thin plywood from hardware store
2. Buy wood glue
    - get type 2, water resistant
    - type 1, waterproof
3. Cut plywood and add glue
4. Clamp together
5. Repeat for each layer

Gorilla glue doesn't have wood types?

## Carbon fiber sheets and epoxy

- much lighter than maple plywood
- CF is higher density (heavier) than wood, which is one reason why wood cores are often used in CF parts


## Trucks

Standard is aluminum cast w/ steel axle inside during cast. 

The higher price options include titanium or magnesium for strength and lightness

-  in terms of weight, usually being within 10% of Al
