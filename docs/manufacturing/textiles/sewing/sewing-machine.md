## Components

![](https://i0.wp.com/slidingmotion.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/Sewing-Machine-Parts-Names-Diagram.png?w=1200&ssl=1)

[Singer 44S, Machine Description, ytb 5min](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=85lbGmErXyU)

- Replacement Bobbins: Singler class 15 

(1) Foot pedal will start the stitching by moving the needle up/down + forward. (2) The large knob on right side of machine does the same. The faster you push the pedal, the faster the sewing.

(3) Adjustable Tension Dial

- above needle on top left
- advised to just leave in center

(4) To raise/lower presser foot

- lever behind the machine near presser foot 
- lower presser foot before sewing
- lift presser when done (park position)

Stich Dials

- (5) length of stitch
    - front top of machine
    - blue stitches only work on s1 length
- (6) stitch type 
    - front bottom (has logo)
- (7) stitch width dial 
    - top right

(8) Center Dial - Change position of needle (just keep at middle for most)

(9) [Adjusable Presser Foot Pressure](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Fgtr2RnGg_I)

- Clockwise, tighten: More pressure for denim/canvas
- Counterclockwise, loosen: Less pressure for thin fabrics
