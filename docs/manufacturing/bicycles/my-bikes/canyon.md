## Components

- 12mm thru-axle
- 9.04 kg (19.9lbs)
- Carbon Fork
- Carbon Seatpost
- 33mm clearance (I fit bigger wheels)
- Cassette: 11/32
- Crankset: Shimano 105, 11S, 52-36 mb (weight: 742.6 g)
- Headtube angle 73*
- Style: lower bottom bracket and longer wheelbase
- Brakes: Shimano RS505 road bike disc brake system
