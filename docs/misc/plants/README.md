# Plants

Plants at home

Midnight Owl (cactus like), Cultivar of the Bird's Nest Snake Plant

- the one got w/ Emron
- grows to only to 6-8 inches
- Watering
    - thoroughly until drains at bottom
    - 2/4 weeks in summer (less in winter)
    - let roots dry completely before water to prevent rot

[Philodendron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philodendron)

- dangeling plant
- Sunlight
    - Bright/indirect sunlight (east/north facing window)
- Watering
    - Every 1/2 weeks for top 1/2 inches to be wet
    - Allow to drain water
- Feed monthly w/ fertilizer
