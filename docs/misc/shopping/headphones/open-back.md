# Open Back Headphones

Back of the earcups open

- leaks more sound out
- lets more ambient sounds into the headphone
- lighter bass

## Impedance 

- High impedance (100 Ω+) → needs a headphone amp for best performance.
_ Lower impedance (<80 Ω) → works easily with phones/laptops.

## Things to look for

- Clamping Pressure
- Lighter is more comfy
- Nice ear cups (not hot, but comfy)
- Detachable cable
- Replaceable earcups

# Ear Cup Material

- Memory foam with velour is common and comfy for long sessions
