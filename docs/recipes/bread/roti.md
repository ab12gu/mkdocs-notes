# Roti

You can grind up grains to make flour at home too!

[grind up sorghum, ytb short](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/TU4Hz7FH0b4)

## Ingredients

- Wheat Flour (atta)
- Water (lukewarm; hotter the water, the more water absorbed (boiled ok))
- Pinch of salt

Add ons:

- chili flakes
- garlic
- cilantro
- Pinch of sugar
- Oil
- 1 tablespoon yogurt for softness

## Steps

1. Mix atta and salt (and add oil?)
2. Periodically add more water to get dough to stick together
3. Mix with fingers
4. Cover and let dough sit for 5-15 minutes
    - idk why
5. Knead the dough for 1 min (with some oil and flour)
6. Break parts of dough out into small balls
7. Put into flour, and roll into tortilla (16-18cm, 7-8in)
8. Add Roti to hot pan (too low and turns into papad?)

## Layered Roti (Lacha Parantha)

- Roll out roti in 1 direction
- Roll up like hotdog style
- Then roll up again into cinnamin roll
- Rest for 30 minutes and flatten + cook
